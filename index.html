IB Reflection Panel

Use evidence from your simulation results to answer these discussion questions. Connect your thinking to the key concept of Efficiency.

QUESTION 01
What does "efficiency" mean in this simulation context? Give a specific real-world example from your results.
Hint: Consider how break-even units changed when you altered fixed vs. variable costs.
QUESTION 02
Which single decision had the most unexpected consequence — and why were you surprised? Use specific data from your results.
Hint: Was it the automation investment or the selling price slider that surprised you most?
QUESTION 03
How would someone from a different cultural or economic background approach these production decisions differently? Consider a small business owner in a developing economy.
Hint: Think about access to capital, labour costs, and market size differences.
QUESTION 04
What would you change if you ran the simulation again? Use specific evidence from your metric results to justify your strategy.
QUESTION 05
How does this simulation connect to a current global issue you know about outside school? (e.g. supply chain disruption, inflation, automation and employment)
QUESTION 06
Which IB Learner Profile attribute did your decision-making best reflect: Thinker or Balanced? Justify with evidence from at least two rounds.
